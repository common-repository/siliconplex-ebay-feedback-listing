== Description ==

Looking forward to integrate your Ebay Feedbacks on your wordpress site "SP Ebay Feedback Listing" does the job for you. 

This plugin integrates your Ebay Store Feedbacks & Reviews to your Wordpress Site with super fast installation process. Now your Feedbacks & Reviews can be easily displayed to any page(s), widget(s) and section(s) of your website with the inclusion of a single Short Code. 



== Installation ==

1- Upload the plugin files to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2- Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ screen in WordPress.
3- Setup your Ebay Account Details in Auth Section of this plugin. 
4- Add ShortCode to any page, widget and section of your page to see the results.  **"[sp_sc_ebay_feedbacks]"**

== Plugin Implementation ==

We developed this plugin in considering a need that users wants to display their eBay feedbacks & reviews on their wordpress site. This plugin fetches the feedback reviews of a user account and display it in your site. During this fetches we follow the authentication process of eBay and communicate to their servers on behave of user.
